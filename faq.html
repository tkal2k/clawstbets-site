FAQ

What is ClawStreetBets?

It's an AI-driven sports betting pipeline and community. A 10-layer automated process sweeps evidence, models edges, sizes bets with discipline, and grades every pick against the closing line. You get the daily output plus a transparent, logged track record.

Is this financial or betting advice?

No. ClawStreetBets is for entertainment and education only. Nothing here is financial, investment, or professional betting advice. We're not licensed advisors. Every decision and bet you make is your own. See our Terms.

What do I get for $39.99/month?

Daily AI sports picks (the L10 digest), the full graded track record and post-mortems, closing-line-value data on every bet, the options/trading screener and crash signals, and access to the Discord community. Cancel anytime.

How are the picks delivered?

Through our Discord server — dedicated channels for each sport (e.g., #nba, #mlb, #worldcup) plus an #alerts channel for time-sensitive plays. After joining via Whop, you get access to the community.

What sports do you cover, and what about the off-season?

Coverage rotates with the calendar — soccer/World Cup, MLB, NBA, WNBA, and more as seasons run. The pipeline focuses where there's a slate; coverage naturally shifts during off-seasons.

Is any of this guaranteed to win?

No. Betting carries real risk and outcomes are never guaranteed. Expect losing bets, losing days, and losing weeks — variance is normal. We show the losses right next to the wins. Only ever risk what you can afford to lose.

How is the track record verified?

Every bet is modeled at a flat stake, logged with timestamps, and graded against the closing line (CLV). We publish wins and losses, disclose sample sizes, and note which books have been net negative — no cherry-picking.

How do I cancel? Are there refunds?

Cancel anytime in your Whop account; it stops future billing and you keep access through the current period. Because it's an instant-access digital membership, payments are non-refundable except for genuine billing errors. See the Refund Policy.

What's the "AI pipeline," really?

A structured L1→L10 process: a nine-lane evidence sweep, market-state classification, a probability/edge model, half-Kelly sizing under hard caps, a daily digest, and a post-mortem that grades results and feeds learnings back in. Same inputs, same output — auditable, not vibes.

Who can join?

You must be at least 18 (or the legal gambling age where you live, if higher) and legally permitted to access this content in your jurisdiction. Know your local laws.

Email info@clawstbets.com or reach out in the Discord.
